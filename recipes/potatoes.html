roasted new red potatoes

Roasted red potatoes at their best — plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

Roasted red potatoes are a simple side dish of red potatoes coated in fat and roasted at high temperature in the oven or a pan until lightly browned and cooked through. They can be customized in many ways using different seasonings, herbs, and fats.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds small red new potatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and adjust the oven rack to the lowest position.
  3. Toss potatoes with oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Arrange, cut-side down, on a rimmed cookie sheet or jellyroll pan.
  5. Roast potatoes in the preheated oven until tender and golden brown, 20 to 30 minutes.
  6. Transfer to a serving dish.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
