lemon-glazed carrots

Lemon-glazed carrots are a very quick and easy recipe to make, that you will enjoy!

These honey-glazed carrots are cooked in a honey, butter, ginger, and lemon glaze. The recipe goes really well with peppercorn steak and cheesecake. The Allrecipes Test Kitchen staff are a team of culinary pros who make and vet recipes using only equipment you'd find in a home kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, sliced 1/4-inch thick
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Place carrots into a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until carrots are tender, about 8 minutes.
  3. Drain.
  4. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Cook and stir carrots, brown sugar, and lemon juice in the melted butter, stirring often, until sugar has dissolved, 2 minutes.
